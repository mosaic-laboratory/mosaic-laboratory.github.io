About Us

Mobile Sensing and Ubiquitous Computing Laboratory

The Mobile Sensing and Ubiquitous Computing Laboratory at the University of California San Diego conducts research on large-scale sensor data systems and multimodal AI/ML for health and behavior modeling. Our focus is on building scalable, energy-efficient sensor data systems through the use of heterogeneous sensor modalities and platforms for solving real-world problems.
