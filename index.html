About Us

Mobile Sensing and Ubiquitous Computing Laboratory

We develop innovative health sensing technologies to translate physical signals into practical insights for better health understanding.

Who we are

We are a team of AI-for-Health researchers led by Prof. Tauhidur Rahman, affiliated with UC San Diego's Halıcıoğlu Data Science Institute and UMass Amherst's Manning College of Information & Computer Science.

Research Interest

We explore novel health sensing technologies that transform acoustic and electromagnetic signals from the body and environment into actionable biological and behavioral insights.

Lab Culture

We foster a collaborative, inclusive, and innovation-driven lab culture that values curiosity and interdisciplinary thinking.

Our Vision

We aim to advance ubiquitous and mobile health sensing to improve lives and revolutionize healthcare accessibility worldwide.
